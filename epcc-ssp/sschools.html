A short list of other SSP-style summer programmes

Here is a short list of other programmes which may be of interest. Except for one instance, we have tried to link only to programmes which have no restrictions on the nationality or religion of applicants, which do not make a charge to apply to the programme, and which pay some form of salary or wage to students.

Disciplines other than computing or `hard science' are included, though most are in some area of `science'.
