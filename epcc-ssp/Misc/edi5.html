Scotland: Page 5
  The clubs and pubs in Edinburgh all have their own unique gimmicks. The lighting in this picture is real. And the giant Jenga tower is also not a figment of your imagination.  
 
  The view from Arthur's Seat is not to be missed. We climbed to the top in the unexpected Scottish weather, and found ourselves standing in the rain at one point. As you can see, the weather got much better as we got closer to the pinnacle of the old volcano.  
 
  Which way home? After having gotten lost within ten meters of the apartment, John and Eurico discuss the finer points of the Edinburgh bus system.  
 
  And finally, the sunset. What better way to end the photo-journey through Scotland?
