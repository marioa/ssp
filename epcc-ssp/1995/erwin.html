I was born on January 29th 1973, and grew up in Terneuzen, The Netherlands, learned how to read and write and lots of other stuff at Openbare Lagere School "De Wiek slag', and came for the first time in contact with physics and computers at that time (though I didn't realize what impact that would have on my later life) and started to learn maths at the 'Petrus Hondius Lyceum'. After graduation in 1991 I went to Eindhoven to study applied physics at the Eindhoven University of Technology. I am now in my 5th year, working on my final subjects and hoping to start my gr aduating project in the first quarter of 1996.
