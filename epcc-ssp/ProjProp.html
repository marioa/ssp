Summer Scholarship Programme Project Proposals for 1997



Have you ever wondered if High Performance Computing (HPC) would help your research? If so, then the Summer Scholarship Program offers a unique opportunity to investigate HPC.

In 1997 12 students will come to work within EPCC for 10 weeks from 7th July 1997. If you have an idea which you think might make a good project for a student to work on then complete this form and send it to the address given.

Note: We can only accept projects from researchers in the Edinburgh region.

The application deadline for project proposals in 1997 is 28th February, 1997.

Please address any queries (by email) to epccssp@epcc.ed.ac.uk or (by fax) to +44 (0)131 650 6555, marked for the attention of the Summer Scholarship Co-ordinator.
