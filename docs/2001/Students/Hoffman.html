Most of my life has been devoted to electrical engineering. But then I wanted to know more about software than hardware and decided to take up computer science studies at Edinburgh University.

My interests range through a lot of the science topics like Physics, Astronomy and Meteorology. But beside these my main leisure time activity is photography and by being a member of the committee of the Photographic Society at Edinburgh University I try to wake this interest in other people too.
