Asimina Sierou was born at 30/5/1973 in Rhodes, an island of Greece. She moved in Pireaus, when she was still very young and she lives there eversince. After finishing High School in 1991, she was accepted at the Chemical Engineering Department of the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA), where she is still studying. She is about to start the 5th and final year of her studies, and she expects to spend the next year of her life working on her Diploma Thesis, which most probably will be in the field of Fluids Dynamics.
