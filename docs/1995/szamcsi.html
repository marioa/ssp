Ákos was born 4th of June, 1973 in Budapest, Hungary. He was attended to High School and University in this city as well.

He is learning at the University of Eötvös Loránd at the Department of General Computer Science in Budapest. He is interested in parallel programming, distibuted systems, and computer graphics.

He reached the degree of Bachelor of Science in Computer Programming and Mathematic in 1994. Now he is working on his Master degree.

He has experience in parallel computing and a few experience in maintaining a parallel computer.
