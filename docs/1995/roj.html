Robert Jackson graduated from Birmingham University, School of Computer Science in 1995 with first class honours in M.Eng. Computer Science and Electrical and Electronic Engineering. He is now working towards a PhD in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering investigating Communications Processors for Heterogenous Parallel Computing. Have a look at his homepage.

His interests include Parallel Computing, Functional Programming, and motor-racing.
