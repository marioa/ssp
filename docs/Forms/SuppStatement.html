Summer Scholarship Programme



Statement of Support

Your Statement of Support should be provided by your director of studies, or equivalent, and should be signed by him/her. NB: Applications cannot be accepted without an accompanying Statement of Support. It is up to you to ensure that we receive your Statement of Support before the closing date.
