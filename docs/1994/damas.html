Sergio Damas Arroyo comes from Granada, Spain. He is studying for a B.S. in Technical Computer Engineering, and is currently enroled in the 4th year of the 5-year Computer Science degree programme at the University of Granada. He is also member of a Research and Development group within this University. After graduation in 1995 he hopes to pursue his doctoral studies in Computer Science.
