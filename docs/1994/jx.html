Jianhong Xu



jx@dcs.ed.ac.uk

Jianhong was born in Shanghai, China, on 8 Jan 1973. He studied Information and Control Engineering in Shanghai Jiao-Tong University during 1991-1993. After transferring to Edinburgh University in 1993, he continued his undergraduate education in the Computer Science and Electronics Joint Honours Degree. Currently he is a third-year student in this course.

His interests range from the computer software to hardware. Favourite areas include parallel computing, computer communication, CAD (Computer Aided Design), computer networking and digital electronics.

Jianhong expects to graduate in June 1996 and intends to continue studying on a postgraduate course in information engineering.
