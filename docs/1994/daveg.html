Since David took part in the Summer Scholarship programe he has graduated from Aberdeen University with a BSc (hons) in Chemistry. His honours project there was an investigatation into the ratios of oxidation states of Molybdenum ions in Sodium doped Molybdophosphate glasses.

After this David undertook a Postgraduate Diploma in Information Technology (Software Engineering) at Napier University. He now works as a systems administrator for Atlantech Technologies, a software development firm specialising in network and network element management software.

You can mail David at dgrierson@atl.co.uk.
