Gordon Henderson is a student of Mathematics with Engineering Technology at Napier University. After graduation in 1995, Gordon is considering an MSc in Numerical Analysis with Computing Technology.

Outwith University life, Gordon has a keen interest in all styles of music and is currently studying jazz piano. He also aims to obtain a teaching certificate for piano after graduation, allowing him to teach the piano in his later years.

As the majority of Computational work at Napier is PC based, working on the SSP has provided Gordon with essential Unix skills which he will not only to be able to apply in any future career developments but also in the near future, in his Honours project.
