David Grierson

I'm currently studying for a BSc in Chemistry at Aberdeen University, my main fields of interest are in solution, physical and computational chemistry.

I became interested in Computing Science in my first year when I did an introduction to computing course; since then I have completed undergraduate computing courses in Programming Methods, Computing with UNIX and Software Engineering for which my project (along with Alastair Slater (ams@csd.abdn.ac .uk)) was a Multi-User Conferencing System for UNIX (TinyMUCS).

On graduation I am hoping to enter the field of computational chemistry, possibly doing an MSc in Software Technology or Information Systems.
