Deborah Bellotti



Deborah was born in Imperia, Italy, on 27 November 1971. She is currently a fourth-year student in Computer Science at the University of Genoa. She studied Computer Science at the University of Edinburgh during the academic year 1993-94 where she attended both fourth and third year courses. Her interests include parallel programming and theoretical computer science. After graduating in 1995, Deborah intends to work in the areas mentioned above.
